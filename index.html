How We Met

Kristine and Lorenzo met at high school and were both in the high school Marching Band. Lorenzo was a senior and had prior marching band experience while Kristine was a junior and had no experience. It was a hot summer day and the high school marching band had just begun their summer practices.
The students were given paper slips that had information on where they were supposed to stand on the football field. Kristine had no idea how to read the slip as did many other new band students who had no prior experience. Students were instructed to find their spots in a timely manner and were told to ask seniors for any help if necessary. Kristine had no idea what she was doing and definitely wanted help from the seniors she knew, but they were all bombarded with other students. She had patiently waited but then realized her instructor had started counting down and if they weren't at their spots in time, they would have to run laps around the field. "Ain't no way in hell I'm doing that," she said.
Kristine looked and looked around and there she found Lorenzo standing with his ginormous Tuba, minding his own business without a care in the world. Kristine went up to Lorenzo and asked "Can you help me find my spot?" Lorenzo looked at her slip and replied "That's easy! It's right next to me." Destined by fate, the whole marching routine revolved around those two being together the whole time. If one was lost, they knew they had to just find each other on the field and as long they were together, Kristine and Lorenzo knew they were in the right place.
